Enjoy Your Stay

Abdalbagi's Hôtels is more than a hotel chain. It's a home-grown family business crafted from the expertise of three generations of the Abdalbagi's family. Abdalbagi's Hôtels owns and operates Le Germain Hotels, Alt Hotels and Alt+ Hotels across Canada. Recognized as one of Canada's 50 Best Managed Companies, it is renowned for its unparalleled hospitality and for the unique style that characterizes its hotels.
