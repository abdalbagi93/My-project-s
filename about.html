About Abdalbagi's Hôtels

Abdalbagi's Hôtels Hotel offers the guests an unforgettable stay with his warm hospitality, unique service quality in the magical nature of mediterranean. Our 5-star hotel close to the historic peninsula of Side and Manavgat, just 12 km away, with his 462 rooms capacity, beachfront location and most importantly with his very friendly service was placed in the short time in hearts of many guests. Fine sand and shallow beach awarded with international prize 'Blue Flag' is waiting just for you. Unlimited hospitality and perfect service awaits you from the moment you step in the Abdalbagi's Hôtels. Full 12 months `All Inclusive' accommodation at your service.
